Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about ordering, trade pricing, and delivery for The Luxe Linen wholesale ranges.

Do you sell wholesale only?

Yes. The Luxe Linen supplies fitted sheets, pillowcases, pillows, toppers, cushions, and bedspreads for retailers, hospitality buyers, and trade partners.

How do I get trade pricing?

Contact us with your business details and the ranges you need. We’ll share wholesale pricing, pack sizes, and current availability.

What product ranges do you supply?

Our core ranges include fitted sheets, pillowcases, V-shape pillows and pillowcases, mattress toppers, filled pillows, filled cushions, booster cushions, and bedspreads.

How long does wholesale delivery take?

Lead times depend on order size and destination. Once your order is confirmed, we’ll confirm dispatch timing and tracking details.

Can I order mixed ranges in one wholesale order?

Yes. Most trade orders combine sheets, pillows, cushions, and related ranges. Tell us what you need and we’ll confirm pack and minimums.
